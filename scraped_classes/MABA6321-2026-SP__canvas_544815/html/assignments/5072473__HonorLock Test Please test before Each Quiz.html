This is for testing the compatability of your device with HonorCode. 

To ensure academic integrity and fairness during online assessments, students must adhere to the following rules:

  1. No External Resources

    • You may not access notes, textbooks, websites, or any other external resources during the quiz unless explicitly permitted by the instructor.

  2. No Communication Tools

    • The use of any communication devices or applications is strictly prohibited during the exam. This includes, but is not limited to:
      • Cell phones
      • Text messaging
      • Instant messaging platforms
      • Email
      • Social media (e.g., Facebook, Snapchat)
      • Collaboration tools (e.g., Slack, Discord)

  3. No Information Sharing

    • You may not share any content or details related to the exam with others at any time, including before, during, or after the assessment.

  4. No Soliciting or Receiving Help

    • You may not seek, solicit, or receive assistance from others regarding the exam content or answers.

The following features of HonorCode will be enabled

  • Recording: video, audio, screen, web traffic
  • Test Taker Photo:  A test taker's photo will be captured before taking the quiz
  • Disabled copy/paste: Clipboard actions will be disabled.
  • Disabled printing: printing of the quiz content will be disabled.
