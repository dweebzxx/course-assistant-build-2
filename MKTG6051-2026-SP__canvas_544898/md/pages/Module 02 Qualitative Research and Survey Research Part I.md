## Module 02: Qualitative Research and Survey Research Part I

### Learning Objectives

* Understand the value of doing qualitative research.
* Learn when and how to use observational research, ethnography/netnography, projective techniques, focus groups, and depth interviews.
* When to use survey (quantitative) research
