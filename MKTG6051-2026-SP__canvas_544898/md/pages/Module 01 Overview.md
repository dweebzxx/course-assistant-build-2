### Additional Resources

The following resources give a little information about tools that can be used to organize group projects with an emphasis on tools that help in asynchronous environments. Importantly, it is a good idea to think about how these tools shape group interactions and how this could affect the final outcome of your project.

* [What Managers Need to Know About Social Tools - HBR](https://hbr.org/2017/11/what-managers-need-to-know-about-social-tools)
* [Make Tools Like Slack Work for Your Company - HBR](https://hbr.org/podcast/2018/02/make-tools-like-slack-work-for-your-company.html)
* [How social tools can reshape the organization - McKinsey](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/mckinsey-digital/our-insights/how-social-tools-can-reshape-the-organization)
